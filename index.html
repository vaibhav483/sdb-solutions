Honest, clear answers to common questions about renting and buying respiratory equipment

Simply call us at 9246243936 or fill our contact form. Our expert will understand your medical needs, recommend the right equipment, and arrange delivery. We collect a small security deposit, deliver the equipment to your home, set it up, and train you free of charge. Monthly rental is collected automatically. Return anytime with just 1 month notice.

Auto CPAP machines start from ₹4,500/month. BiPAP machines start from ₹5,500/month. Oxygen concentrators start from ₹4,000/month. Portable oxygen concentrators start from ₹6,000/month. Prices may vary by model and rental duration. Call us for a specific quote for your requirement.

For CPAP and BiPAP machines, a doctor's prescription with recommended pressure settings is needed for optimal therapy. For oxygen concentrators, a prescription with required LPM (litres per minute) is required. If you don't have one, we can help you get a Home Sleep Study done to get a diagnosis and prescription from our partner doctors.

Yes, 100% free delivery and professional setup. Our certified technician delivers the equipment, installs it, tests it, and provides thorough hands-on training to you and your family on how to use it correctly. All maintenance, servicing, and repairs are fully included in the rental price — at no extra cost. Simply call our 24/7 helpline and we will send a technician or replace the unit within the shortest possible time. For sale customers, equipment comes with manufacturer warranty and we provide extended service support.

Yes! We offer flexible rental starting from just 1 month. This is perfect for post-surgery recovery, COVID recovery, short-term therapy trials, or any temporary need. Simply call us and we can arrange a 1-month or longer rental with no long-term commitment required.

If you've purchased equipment from us and want to upgrade, we buy it back at up to 70% of the original purchase price, depending on the age and condition of the equipment. This makes upgrading to newer, better technology very affordable. Call us with your equipment details and we'll give you a valuation.

Yes! We serve all 33 districts of Telangana. Same-day delivery is available within Hyderabad.
