About This Product

What is BiPAP?

BiPAP (Continuous Positive Airway Pressure) is the gold standard for treating obstructive sleep apnea. Unlike fixed-pressure devices, BiPAP automatically monitors and adjusts air pressure throughout the night — providing optimal therapy with maximum comfort and compliance.

✅ Best For

  • • Patients who cannot tolerate CPAP therapy
  • • Individuals needing higher pressure support for breathing
  • • Patients with COPD, central sleep apnea, or respiratory disorders
  • • Those who experience difficulty exhaling against pressure
  • • Patients requiring both inhalation and exhalation pressure support

✅ Key Benefits

  • • Dual pressure system (IPAP & EPAP) for easier breathing
  • • More comfortable for patients with high pressure needs
  • • Helps reduce breathing effort and improves oxygen intake
  • • Effective for complex sleep apnea and lung conditions
  • • Advanced monitoring with detailed therapy data tracking
